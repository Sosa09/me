About Me

My journey started with a fascination for the basics – understanding how Windows systems and hardware actually work. Knowing the foundations is key to building things that don't fall over.

Discovering DevOps felt like finding the missing piece. It lets me combine everything I enjoy: system administration, networking, automating things, and even a bit of development, all in one role.

When people say I'm 'happy to work,' it's really about my mindset. A broken system isn't a disaster, it's a puzzle I get to solve. I genuinely enjoy the process of digging in, figuring out the 'why,' and getting things running smoothly again. That's the energy I bring to every challenge.
